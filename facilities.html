Facilities

Spacious Classrooms

Our classrooms are spacious, well-lit, and equipped with modern teaching aids to facilitate effective learning.

Computer Labs

We have fully equipped computer labs that enable hands-on learning and experimenting with new technology.

Library

Our library is a treasure trove of knowledge, offering a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to cultivate a love for reading and research.

Sports Facilities

We recognize the importance of physical fitness and offer various sports facilities, including a playground, basketball court, and indoor sports area. We also conduct several sports competitions which are held in sports week.

Art and Yoga

Our art and yoga facility provide students with a creative space to explore their artistic talents and express themselves through various art forms.

Smart Teachings

We embrace technology and have smart teachings with interactive whiteboards and audio-visual aids, enhancing the learning experience.

Auditorium

Our well-equipped auditorium serves as a venue for school events, assemblies, and cultural programs.

Transport Services

We offer safe and reliable transport services, ensuring convenient commuting for students.

Guidance and Counseling

Our guidance and counseling services provide support and mentorship to students, helping them navigate academic and personal challenges.

Safety and Security

Ensuring the safety and security of our students is our top priority. We have robust security measures in place to create a safe learning environment.

At Ideal English School, we believe that providing exceptional facilities is integral to nurturing well-rounded individuals. Our facilities complement our rigorous academic program, allowing students to explore their interests, passions, and potential. We strive to create an environment that encourages curiosity, instills values, and prepares students to become compassionate and responsible global citizens. Come and experience the array of facilities that await you at Ideal English School. We look forward to providing your child with an enriching and inspiring educational journey.
