Academics

Welcome to the Academics section of Ideal English School! We take immense pride in providing a transformative and enriching educational experience that nurtures young minds and prepares them for a future of endless possibilities.

Dedicated Faculty:

Our dedicated team of 20 educators comprises passionate professionals who are experts in their respective fields. They go above and beyond to inspire and engage students, creating a positive and conducive learning environment. We believe in the power of personalized attention, and our faculty ensures that each student receives the support and guidance they need to thrive academically.
Academic Curriculum:

For nursery to VIII, our curriculum is carefully designed to be holistic and dynamic considering Maharashtra state board syllabus, catering to the unique learning needs of each student. We follow a blend of traditional teaching methodologies and innovative approaches that foster critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and creativity.

External Scholarships:

We provide scholarship exams assistance to students for their overall development other than regular syllabus and career growth. The preparation for scholarship exams like Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test, Maharashtra Tallent Search Exam, olympiad exams etc. We have toppers holding rank 1 and 2 from parola taluka.
